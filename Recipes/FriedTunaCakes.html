Tuna Fried Cakes

Delicious for a snack alone or served with some garlic rice!

Very yummy, made with simple ingredient and extremely fast to make, your family and KIDS WILL LOVE IT!


Ingredient:

  • 4 cans of tuna
  • 2 big handfuls of spinach
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • Salt and black pepper

Steps:

  1. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl
  2. Add salt and black pepper to likings
  3. Pour some oil onto your pan and wait for it to heat up
  4. Using a scooper, scoop some of the mixture out, and drop into medium-hot pan
  5. Fry on both sides until golden brown
  6. Bon Apetite!
