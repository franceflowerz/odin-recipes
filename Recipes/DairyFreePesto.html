Dairy Free Pesto

Pesto that won't send you to the toilet!

This pesto will have you drooling for more! I am not responsible for any pesto addictions! haha


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cashews
  • 3 cups packed basil
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 3/4 cup of olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper

Steps:

  1. Blend all ingredients
  2. Sautee in pan for a few minutes, stirring occasionally
  3. Add more oil if it gets dry
  4. serve over pasta of choice
  5. ENJOY!

Don't forget to freeze any leftovers! Stays fresh for up to 3 months!
