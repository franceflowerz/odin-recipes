Lomo Saltado

The Famous Lomo Saltado!!!

In this recipe, you will learn to cook like a pro! This is one of those dishes that you want to make for your loved ones on a special occasion!

Plus, who does'nt love french fries and rice?! Steak, Soy sauce and sauteed onions?! Anyways! Lets get started with what we'll need for this mouthwatering recipe!

Ingredients:/h3>
  • 1lb Choice Of Steak(sliced)
  • 1 Whole Red Onion(thick slices)
  • 2 Tomatoes(thick slices)
  • 4 Garlics(finely chopped)
  • Siyau(soy sauce)
  • Black Pepper
  • 4 Potatoes(chopped into thick french fries)
  • Cooked Rice

Steps:

  1. Start by frying your french fries, set aside in oven on low heat to maintain it's form, texture, and warmth
  2. Black pepper your steak and start to sautee on medium heat and give it a nice sear on both sides
  3. Start adding the garlic and sautee together for 2 minutes
  4. Add the chopped onions and tomatoes and sautee everything together, and little by little start to add the Siyau(soy sauce) to your likings!
  5. once you see its fully cooked, serve rice on a plate, add the french fries next to the rice, and add the Lomo Saltado RIGHT ON TOP OF THE FRIES!

p.s Dont forget to pour some of the juices on the pan on top of your rice and french fries for an even more flavorful bite ;)
